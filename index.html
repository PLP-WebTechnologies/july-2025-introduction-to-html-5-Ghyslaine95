HTML5 Definition

HTML (HyperText Markup Language): It first appeared in 1991 with the launch of the Web.
Its role is to manage and organize content. Therefore, it's in HTML that you write what should be displayed on the page: text, links, images, etc.
For example, you could say: "This is my title, this is my menu, here is the main text of the page, here is an image to display, etc."

HTML5 tags

HTML pages are filled with what are called tags. These are invisible onscreen to your visitors, but they allow the computer to understand what to display.

What are they for? They indicate the nature of the text they surround. For example, they mean:
"This is the title of the page," "This is an image," "This is a paragraph of text," etc.

SEO Optimization

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the practice of improving a website's visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) to attract more organic (unpaid) traffic.
It involves optimizing various aspects of a website, including its content, structure, and technical elements, to make it more appealing to both users and search engines.
